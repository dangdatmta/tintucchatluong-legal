Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 21, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data VidGenn collects, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

VidGenn is an AI-powered video generation and publishing platform available at auto-gen-vid.vercel.app. It allows content creators to create short-form video series and automatically publish them to social media platforms including TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the platform:

Category What we collect Why
Account Email address, name, profile photo (via OAuth login) To create and manage your account
Billing Payment method details (processed by Stripe — we do not store card numbers) To manage subscriptions and credits
Social Auth TikTok OAuth access token, refresh token, and basic creator info (username, avatar, privacy options) To publish videos to your connected TikTok account on your behalf
Content Series settings (niche, voice, style, captions), generated video files, publish schedules To generate and deliver your video series
Usage Feature interactions, credit usage, error logs To improve the platform and debug issues

3. TikTok Integration — Specific Disclosures

When you connect your TikTok account to VidGenn, we request the following permissions via TikTok's official API:

  • user.info.basic — to retrieve your TikTok display name, avatar, and posting eligibility, shown in your VidGenn dashboard
  • video.upload — to upload generated video files to TikTok on your behalf
  • video.publish — to publish uploaded videos to your TikTok profile according to your schedule

We store your TikTok OAuth tokens securely and use them solely to post content you have configured. We do not read, store, or analyze your TikTok followers, messages, or engagement data. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time from VidGenn Settings.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and deliver the VidGenn service
  • To authenticate you and manage your account
  • To generate AI video content based on your series configuration
  • To publish content to your connected social media accounts as scheduled
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (account, billing, and generation status notifications)
  • To improve platform performance and fix bugs

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only with the following categories of service providers, strictly to operate the platform:

  • TikTok, YouTube, Instagram — to publish content via their official APIs
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Vercel / cloud infrastructure — for hosting and storage
  • AI generation services — to generate video content (data is not retained by these services beyond processing)

We do not sell, rent, or broker your personal data to any third party.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Generated video files are retained for a limited period (30 days) after generation, after which they are deleted from our servers unless you have explicitly saved them. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage of OAuth tokens, HTTPS-only data transmission, and role-based access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Receive a portable copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via our platform or the details below.

9. Cookies

VidGenn uses essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling essential cookies will affect platform functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

VidGenn is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of VidGenn after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Privacy questions or data requests?

Contact us at auto-gen-vid.vercel.app via our in-app support chat. We respond to all privacy inquiries within 7 business days.
